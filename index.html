Who are we? 

We are a group of technology enthusiasts who came together under the auspices of the INQb University Technology Incubator with a common goal: to help European industry.

How we started?

This is how the DAITABLE project (formerly Green Deal Solutions) was born as a response to the need of industrial companies to optimize their energy costs and to make their production more efficient in terms of the new green economy. Currently, as a young, aggressive and promising startup, we have the ambition to provide companies with detailed data with our IT systems based on artificial intelligence, with which they will be able to make better decisions and thus achieve better results.
